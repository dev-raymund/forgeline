# Web Development Studio — Services & Client Plan

> Zero-budget plan to land clients for **Forgeline Technologies** — your web development studio.
> Every step here is free. Swap in your real studio name, stack, and prices as you go.

## 1. Positioning (your one sentence)

> **We design and build websites, Shopify stores, and custom web apps for businesses and founders who want it done right — fixed scope, clear pricing.**

This goes on your site, your GitHub bio, your LinkedIn, and every proposal. One clear promise, one type of client.

> ✏️ Lead with your proof. Strong starting lines: "We build conversion-focused Shopify stores," or "Custom WordPress sites for growing businesses." (Solo? "We" is still fine — most studios are small.)

## 2. Who you serve (ICP)

Lead with the ones you can win fastest using your WordPress + Shopify proof:

- **Small businesses & local shops** needing a website (WordPress) or an online store (Shopify).
- **E-commerce brands / founders** launching or upgrading a Shopify store.
- **Agencies** needing white-label WordPress/Shopify dev capacity.
- *(Growing into)* **Founders / startups** who need a **custom web app** beyond a CMS.

## 3. Your products (productized services)

Packaged offers with fixed scope — the same ones on your site. Lead with **Store Launch** for e-commerce clients, **Site Sprint** for businesses needing a website.

| Product | Price (starting) | What they get |
|---|---|---|
| **Site Sprint** | from $800 | WordPress or static website. Custom theme, responsive, deployed. ~1–2 weeks. |
| **Store Launch** ⭐ | from $2,500 | Full Shopify store — custom theme, products, payments, launch-ready. 2–4 weeks. |
| **Build Partner** (retainer) | from $1,500/mo | Ongoing updates, features, and theme work. Priority support, cancel anytime. |
| **Care Plan** | from $150/mo | Hosting, backups, updates, small fixes. Recurring revenue that keeps clients close. |

**Also available:** Custom Web App (from $4,000) · Speed & SEO Tune-up (from $400) · Migration & Redesign (from $600).

### Pricing strategy (start lean, raise fast)

These are **starting** numbers — deliberately approachable so you can close your first clients without flinching. The plan is to climb from here:

- **Founding rate:** take your first 2–3 clients at these prices in exchange for a testimonial, then **raise 20–40% each time you close one.** Studios go from a $2,500 store to $6k+ builds within a year doing exactly this.
- **A project price is weeks of work, not a salary.** A $2,500 Shopify store over ~3 weeks is ~$830/week — a normal weekly wage for skilled work. The client compares it to hiring an agency (far more), so to them it's cheap. Quote it without flinching.
- **You're doing both markets — quote each in its own currency:**
  - **International (US / EU / AU):** quote in **USD** at these "from" numbers. They're lean for that market, so you have room to go *up* as proof grows.
  - **Local PH clients:** quote in **₱ (PHP)** on the call. The low end maps to what local SMEs can handle — e.g. a WordPress site ~₱25k–₱45k, a Shopify store ~₱120k–₱250k. Scope smaller if needed.
- **Always quote per project after a free scoping call.** The "from" pricing + the call give you room to flex per client and market. Never commit to a number cold.

### How you start with no testimonials

- Take your **first 1–2 projects at a "founding" rate** (discounted or a smaller fixed scope) **in exchange for a testimonial + permission to show the work** in your portfolio.
- Or build **one polished demo project** (a real-looking app) you fully own and can show. That *is* your proof until clients give you theirs.

## 4. Proof plan (your credibility)

1. **This studio site** — your services + work in one link.
2. **Live project links** — your real WordPress sites and Shopify stores are your strongest proof. Get a screenshot + live URL for each.
3. **1–2 short case studies** — the business, what you built, the result. Even a small store counts.
4. **Testimonials** — ask the past clients whose sites/stores you built, plus your first founding clients.
5. *(For custom work)* a clean **GitHub** with 1–2 demo repos as you grow into custom builds.

## 5. Go-to-market: landing clients with $0

Do **a few of these consistently** — no ad spend required.

**A. Freelance platforms (fastest to first paid work)**
- **Upwork**, **Contra** (no fees), **Wellfound** (startup contract roles), **Toptal** (apply if senior).
- Write a sharp profile around your positioning; apply to 5–10 relevant jobs/day with *tailored* proposals.

**B. Direct outreach (daily, ~10/day)**
- Find businesses with weak/outdated sites or new Shopify stores (local business directories, Instagram shops, LinkedIn, **Indie Hackers**, **X/Twitter**).
- Personalized message: point out one thing you'd improve on their site/store, low-friction offer ("happy to do a free 20-min review call").

**C. Communities (be the helpful dev)**
- `r/forhire`, `r/shopify`, `r/Wordpress`, **WordPress/Shopify Facebook groups**, local business groups, relevant **Discords/Slacks**.
- Answer questions genuinely. People hire the dev who clearly knows WordPress/Shopify.

**D. Build in public**
- Post what you build on **X / LinkedIn** — screenshots, short demos, lessons. Devs get inbound from this.

**E. Warm network + local**
- Tell people you're taking on website & Shopify projects. Offer a build to a local business or shop that needs a site or online store.

**F. The free hook**
- Offer a **free technical scoping call** (or a quick free fix/audit). Free to give, builds trust, starts the deal.
