## Packages overview

Northlight Studio offers three productized packages. All are month-to-month after an initial 90-day commitment. Prices are starting points; exact scope is confirmed on a call.

## Launch — from $1,500/month

For founders who need to get visible consistently without doing it themselves.

- 1 strategy + messaging session to lock your core message
- 8 short-form social posts per month, written in your voice
- Light monthly review of what's working

Best for: personal brands and solo founders just getting serious about content.

## Growth — from $3,500/month

Our most popular package. A full content engine.

- Everything in Launch
- 12–16 posts per month across one primary platform (usually LinkedIn or Instagram)
- 1 long-form piece per month (newsletter, article, or video script) repurposed into shorts
- A simple lead magnet + nurture email sequence
- Monthly metrics review focused on conversations and leads booked

Best for: founder-led businesses doing $10k–$200k/month who want a real, compounding presence.

## Partner — from $6,500/month

Done-with-you growth partnership for brands ready to scale.

- Everything in Growth
- Multi-platform content + repurposing
- Outbound support (targeted DM/email playbooks)
- Funnel and offer guidance
- Priority access and direct line to the founder

Best for: established brands that want marketing handled end-to-end with a strategic partner.

## What we don't do

- Paid ads management (we'll refer you to a trusted partner)
- One-off "post a few things" gigs — we work on systems, not band-aids
- Long lock-in contracts — after the first 90 days it's month-to-month
