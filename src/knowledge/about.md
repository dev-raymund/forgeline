# About the founder and agency

I'm Raymund, founder of Northlight Studio. I help founder-led brands turn attention into revenue with content, brand, and social that actually sounds like them.

I started Northlight after years of watching good businesses get buried under generic, lifeless marketing. My take: most brands don't have a marketing problem, they have a *clarity* problem. Once the message is sharp, everything downstream gets easier.

# Who we work best with

- Founder-led service businesses and personal brands doing $10k–$200k/month who know they should be visible but don't have the time or system to do it consistently.
- Coaches, consultants, agencies, and creators who want their content to sound like a real person, not a committee.

# Who we're NOT for

- Businesses looking for the cheapest possible option or a "post 30 reels and hope" volume play.
- Anyone who wants us to fully ghostwrite without ever being involved — the best work is collaborative.

# Positioning

We help founder-led brands sound unmistakably like themselves and convert attention into clients — without you living inside the content treadmill.

# What makes us different

- Voice-first: we capture how you actually talk and build everything from that.
- Strategy before output: one clear message, then content that compounds.
- We measure what matters — leads and conversations booked, not vanity likes.
