## How does onboarding work?

We start with a 90-minute voice + strategy session to capture how you talk and lock your core message. From there we build a content plan and you approve a batch before anything goes out. First posts usually go live within 2 weeks.

## How involved do I need to be?

Lightly but meaningfully. We need ~30–45 minutes from you per month (a quick voice note or call) plus async approvals. We do the heavy lifting; your fingerprints stay on the work.

## How do you make it sound like me and not AI?

Voice-first process: we study how you actually talk, build a voice guide, and you review early drafts closely until it's dialed in. Nothing goes out that doesn't sound like you.

## How soon will I see results?

Consistency compounds. Most clients see meaningfully more profile views, inbound conversations, and DMs within 60–90 days. We track conversations and leads booked, not just likes. We don't guarantee specific numbers — anyone who does is lying.

## What's the commitment?

An initial 90-day commitment so the work has time to compound, then month-to-month. No long lock-in.

## Do you do paid ads / web design / video editing?

Our core is content, brand voice, and social. We handle light video scripting and repurposing. For paid ads or full production we bring in trusted partners.

## What does it cost?

Launch starts at $1,500/mo, Growth at $3,500/mo, Partner at $6,500/mo. Exact scope and price are confirmed on a quick call.
