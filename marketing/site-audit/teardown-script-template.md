# Teardown script template (~2 min)

Reusable narration for your free-teardown Loom. Run `node audit.mjs <url>`, open the
report, and drop its top 3 findings into the blanks below. Keep it warm and helpful —
you're giving value, not selling. Show each issue on screen as you talk.

---

**[0:00–0:10 — Intro]**
"Hey [NAME], Raymund here from Forgeline. I came across [BUSINESS] and took a quick look
at your site — really like [ONE GENUINE POSITIVE]. I spotted 3 quick things that could
help it bring in more [customers / bookings / sales], so I recorded this for you."

**[0:10–0:40 — Issue 1]**  *(your #1 finding)*
"First — [ISSUE 1]. [Show it.] Here's why it matters: [WHY IT COSTS THEM]. The fix is
[THE FIX] — usually pretty quick."

**[0:40–1:10 — Issue 2]**  *(your #2 finding)*
"Second — [ISSUE 2]. [Show it, e.g. resize to mobile.] [WHY IT COSTS THEM]. [THE FIX]."

**[1:10–1:40 — Issue 3]**  *(your #3 finding)*
"Third — [ISSUE 3]. [Show it.] [WHY IT COSTS THEM]. [THE FIX]."

**[1:40–2:00 — Close]**
"Those are three quick wins. If you'd like, I can take care of them for you — happy to
jump on a quick 15-minute call and scope it out. Either way, hope this was useful. Cheers!"

---

### Delivery tips
- Webcam bubble ON (people trust a face). Don't script word-for-word — talk like a human.
- Show, don't just tell: scroll, resize to phone width, point at the slow bit.
- Under ~2.5 min. Don't over-edit. Send the raw Loom link.
- Map severity → order: lead with the 🔴 findings; they're the most visible/persuasive.
